LifLif Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

LifLif (“we”, “our”, “the App”) respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use LifLif mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number
  • Name and address for delivery
  • Location (for delivery and rider navigation)
  • Order details

How We Use Your Information

  • To complete and deliver your orders
  • To improve app performance
  • To provide customer support
  • To enhance security and prevent fraud

Data Sharing

We only share necessary data with delivery riders and partner vendors to complete your orders. We do not sell your data.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information.
