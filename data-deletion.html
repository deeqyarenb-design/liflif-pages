User Data Deletion for LifLif

Last updated: November 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

If you want to delete your account or request that we remove any personal data associated with your LifLif account, you can follow these steps:

  1. Email us at lilif942@gmail.com with the subject line: “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include your full name, phone number, and email used on the LifLif app.
  3. We will process your deletion request within 48–72 hours.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

We will delete the following user data:

  • Account profile information
  • Order history
  • Saved addresses
  • Payment-related data (if applicable)

What Data Might Be Retained?

Some data may be kept for legal, fraud-prevention, or financial record purposes.

Need Help?

You can contact us anytime at lilif942@gmail.com.
